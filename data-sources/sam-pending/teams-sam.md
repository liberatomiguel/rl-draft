# RLCS SAM (South America) — Top 8 per season — ADDITIVE teams to merge

> Generated for the **SAM Only** regional mode (next patch). These are the SAM
> Top-8 teams that did **NOT** reach RLCS Worlds/Finals — every block carries
> `flag: sam-only`, so they must be EXCLUDED from the general draft and shown
> only in the SAM regional pool. Teams that already reached Worlds/Finals are
> ALREADY in `data-sources/teams.md` and are **not** repeated here (see the
> per-season tables in `sam-merge-notes.md`).
>
> Format is identical to `teams.md` (so it slots straight into the `build:data`
> pipeline) **plus** one extra line per block: `flag: sam-only`. The current
> generator ignores unknown lines, so this is harmless until the patch wires it
> up — see `sam-merge-notes.md` for the exact generator change + the dedup map
> + the COUNTRY-map additions + the overall methodology.
>
> SAM debuted in S7. Definitions: S7–S9 = the 8 Grand Series teams (= the
> season Top 8). RLCS X onward = the Top 8 of the final SAM regional ranking.
> 2026 is omitted (season in progress, no final ranking yet).
